PostgreSQL 9.4.3, 9.3.8, 9.2.12, 9.1.17 & 9.0.21 Released!

4th June 2015

The PostgreSQL Global Development Group is pleased to announce the availability of PostgreSQL 9.4.3, 9.3.8, 9.2.12, 9.1.17 & 9.0.21!

These new releases contain bug fixes over previous releases. All users should plan to upgrade their systems as soon as possible.
